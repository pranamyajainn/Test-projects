Maintaining Professional Balance

✅ Do's

  • • Use self-deprecating humor
  • • Make fun of situations, not people
  • • Always include educational value
  • • Keep it clean and professional
  • • Use relatable analogies

❌ Don'ts

  • • Mock client situations
  • • Use controversial humor
  • • Compromise compliance
  • • Over-share personal details
  • • Forget your professional image

💡 Pro Tips

  • • Test humor on colleagues first
  • • Mix comedy with serious posts
  • • Monitor engagement patterns
  • • Stay updated with trends
  • • Build your unique voice
